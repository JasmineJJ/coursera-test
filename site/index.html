Sushi

Sushi is a type of Japanese dish that typically consists of vinegared rice topped with raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, and other ingredients. It is often served with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger. Popular types of sushi include nigiri (small pieces of fish or other toppings pressed onto a bed of rice), maki (sushi rolls wrapped in nori seaweed), and sashimi (raw fish served without rice).
